## Sub-labeling Convention

Beams from R1 repeat at multiple locations. Sub-labels assigned based on position in Plan at Top:

| Sub-label | Original | Orientation | Position / Runs between |

| B1a | B1 | Horizontal | C1 to right wall (upper horizontal grid line) |
| B1b | B1 | Horizontal | C2 to right wall (lower horizontal grid line) |
| B1c | B1 | Horizontal | **NEW** - C1 to left wall (same line as B1a, extends left from C1) |
| B2a | B2 | Vertical | Top wall down to C1 |
| B2b | B2 | Horizontal | Left wall to C2 (short beam) |
| B3a | B3 | Vertical | C1 to C2 (between the two columns) |
| B3b | B3 | Vertical | Wall to wall, parallel to B3a on the right side |
| B4 | B4 | Vertical | C2 going south into lower extension |
